<p><p>The paint booth filter market caters to two primary applications: dry paint booths and wet paint booths. Dry paint booths utilize filters to capture overspray and particulate matter from solvent-based paints, ensuring a clean environment and compliance with air quality regulations. In contrast, wet paint booths employ water-based filtration systems that trap overspray in a liquid medium, enhancing efficiency and reducing VOC emissions. Both applications prioritize worker safety, environmental protection, and operational efficiency in industrial painting processes.</p></p>
