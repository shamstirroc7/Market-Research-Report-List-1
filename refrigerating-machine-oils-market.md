<p><strong>The Refrigerating Machine Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mineral Oil</li><li>Synthetic Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerating machine oils market is primarily divided into two types: mineral oils and synthetic oils. Mineral oils, derived from refining crude oil, offer good lubrication and thermal stability but may have limitations in low-temperature performance and environmental impact. Synthetic oils, on the other hand, are man-made and provide superior thermal efficiency, low-temperature adaptability, and reduced environmental concerns. Their longer lifespan and better performance in extreme conditions make synthetic oils increasingly preferred in modern refrigeration applications.</p></p>
