<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Screw-type Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-injected</li><li>Oil-free (including Dry type and Water jet type)</li><li>Liquid Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screw-type compressors are categorized by their lubrication methods. Oil-injected compressors use oil to lubricate the screws, enhancing efficiency and cooling but can contaminate the output air. Oil-free compressors, including dry-type and water jet types, offer cleaner air by eliminating oil in the compression process. Dry-type compressors rely on alternative materials for lubrication, while water jet types use water to cool and lubricate, ensuring no oil contamination. Liquid injection systems introduce a liquid to assist with cooling and lubrication, ensuring effective performance.</p></p>
