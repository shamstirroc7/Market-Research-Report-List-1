<p><p>The vehicle optronic market encompasses multispectral and hyperspectral technologies, each serving distinct purposes. Multispectral imaging captures data across a limited number of spectral bands, enabling effective target detection and classification in various environments. Hyperspectral imaging, on the other hand, collects information from a much broader range of wavelengths, allowing for detailed material analysis and identification. Both technologies enhance situational awareness and reconnaissance capabilities in military and civilian applications, contributing to improved decision-making and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
