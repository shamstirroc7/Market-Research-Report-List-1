<p><ul><li>Mechanical Turbocharging</li><li>Exhaust Turbocharging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Turbocharger for Commercial Vehicles market comprises two main types: mechanical turbocharging and exhaust turbocharging. Mechanical turbocharging utilizes a belt-driven compressor to enhance engine performance by forcing more air into the combustion chamber, leading to improved efficiency. In contrast, exhaust turbocharging uses exhaust gas flow to spin a turbine, boosting air intake without additional engine load. Both types aim to increase power output, enhance fuel efficiency, and reduce emissions in commercial vehicles, thus meeting stringent regulatory standards and performance demands.</p></p>
