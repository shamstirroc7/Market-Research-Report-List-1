<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Tissue Market is primarily segmented into two types based on the manufacturing process: Wet-Laid and Dry-Laid processes. The Wet-Laid process involves mixing glass fibers with water and additives, creating a slurry that is then formed into sheets. This method offers uniformity and versatility in applications. In contrast, the Dry-Laid process uses dry fibers that are air-laid onto a substrate, resulting in a denser product. Both processes cater to varied applications, including construction, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
