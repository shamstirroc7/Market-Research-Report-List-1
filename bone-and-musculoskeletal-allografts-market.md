<p><strong>The Bone and Musculoskeletal Allografts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corticocancellous Allografts</li><li>Massive Structural Allografts</li><li>Osteoinductive Allografts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone and musculoskeletal allografts market comprises three primary types: corticocancellous allografts, massive structural allografts, and osteoinductive allografts. Corticocancellous allografts feature both cortical and cancellous bone, providing structural support and facilitating healing. Massive structural allografts are larger bone segments used for substantial reconstruction, especially in trauma or tumor cases. Osteoinductive allografts contain properties that stimulate new bone formation, promoting healing in complex vascular or spinal surgeries. Each type serves specific clinical needs in orthopedic and reconstructive procedures.</p></p>
