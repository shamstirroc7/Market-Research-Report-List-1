<p><p>The Subway Sliding Door System Market is divided into two main types: Spindle and Toothbelt Unit systems. Spindle systems utilize a central rotating spindle mechanism to control door movement, providing smooth operation and compact design. In contrast, Toothbelt Unit systems employ a toothed belt that drives the doors, allowing for precise positioning and quiet performance. Both types are essential for enhancing passenger accessibility and safety in subway systems, catering to varying operational requirements and technological preferences in urban transit environments.</p></p>
