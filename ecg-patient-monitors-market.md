<p><p>The ECG patient monitors market is categorized into three main types: DC type, AC type, and DC & AC type. DC type monitors utilize direct current for signal amplification, offering high precision but limited range. AC type monitors leverage alternating current, providing a broader range of applications, especially in dynamic environments. DC & AC type monitors combine the strengths of both, ensuring versatility and robustness in monitoring heart activity across various healthcare settings, enhancing patient care and diagnostics.</p></p>
