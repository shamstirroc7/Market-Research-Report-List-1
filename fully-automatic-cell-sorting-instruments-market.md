<p><p>The fully automatic cell sorting instruments market comprises mainly two types: Flow Cytometry Sorters and Magnetic Cell Sorting Instruments. Flow Cytometry Sorters utilize lasers and fluorescence to analyze and separate cells based on specific characteristics, allowing for high-throughput sorting with precision. In contrast, Magnetic Cell Sorting Instruments employ magnetically tagged antibodies to isolate targeted cells from a mixed population, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution. Both technologies enhance research and clinical applications in immunology, oncology, and cell therapy.</p></p>
