Manufactured Gas, historically produced from coal or oil, has largely been phased out due to safety and efficiency concerns. LPG, derived from natural gas processing or oil refining, consists mainly of propane and butane, serving as a versatile energy source for heating, cooking, and vehicles.</p></p>
