<p><strong>The Rubber Carbon Black Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Black</li><li>Furnace Black</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rubber Carbon Black market encompasses various types, primarily thermal black and furnace black. Thermal black is produced through the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons, resulting in larger particles and lower surface area, making it suitable for applications requiring good reinforcement and low abrasion. In contrast, furnace black is generated through the combustion of hydrocarbons in a controlled environment, yielding finer particles with higher surface area, ideal for enhancing strength, durability, and performance in rubber products, particularly tires.</p></p>
