<p><strong>The Contactless Payment Terminals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>Near field communication</li><li>Bluetooth</li><li>Radio-frequency identification</li><li>Carrier-based mobile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contactless payment terminals utilize various technologies for transactions. Infrared systems require direct line-of-sight, while Near Field Communication (NFC) enables transactions within a few centimeters, making it popular for smartphones. Bluetooth facilitates payments over longer distances, enhancing user convenience. Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) uses electromagnetic fields to identify and track tags attached to objects, allowing seamless interactions. Lastly, the carrier-based mobile market leverages telecom networks for transactions, enabling payment through mobile devices without direct contact, enhancing accessibility and ease of use.</p></p>
