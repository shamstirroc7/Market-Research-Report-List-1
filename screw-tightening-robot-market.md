<p><ul><li>Cartesian Robot</li><li>SCARA Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw tightening robot market is primarily segmented into Cartesian and SCARA robots. Cartesian robots utilize a linear movement system, ideal for precise vertical and horizontal operations, making them suitable for bulk screw assembly tasks. In contrast, SCARA robots feature a dual-arm design that allows for radial movement, excelling in assembly applications requiring speed and flexibility. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in screw tightening processes, catering to various industrial automation needs.</p></p>
