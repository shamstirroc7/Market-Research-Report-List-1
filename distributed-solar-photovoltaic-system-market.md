<p><p>The distributed solar photovoltaic system market is primarily categorized into rooftop and ground solar photovoltaic systems. Rooftop solar systems involve installing solar panels on residential or commercial rooftops, utilizing existing structures to generate electricity, often promoting self-consumption and reducing energy bills. In contrast, ground solar photovoltaic systems are installed on open land, suitable for larger-scale energy production. These systems can serve utility needs and larger battery storage, capitalizing on higher energy output potential due to unobstructed sunlight exposure.</p></p>
