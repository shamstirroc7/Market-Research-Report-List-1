<p><p>The Tunnel Field Effect Transistor (TFET) market is segmented into lateral and vertical tunneling types. Lateral tunneling TFETs operate by facilitating electron movement parallel to the substrate, allowing for integration in high-density applications. In contrast, vertical tunneling TFETs enable current flow perpendicular to the substrate, offering benefits in terms of scalability and power efficiency. Both types are gaining traction for their potential in low-power electronics, enhancing performance in applications like mobile devices and IoT technologies.</p></p>
