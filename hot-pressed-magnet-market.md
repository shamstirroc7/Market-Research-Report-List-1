<p><ul><li>Hot-Pressed Isotropic Neodymium Iron Boron Magnet</li><li>Hot Extrusion Anisotropic Neodymium Iron Boron Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot-pressed magnet market is divided into two main types: hot-pressed isotropic neodymium iron boron magnets and hot-extrusion anisotropic neodymium iron boron magnets. Isotropic magnets are characterized by uniform magnetic properties in all directions, allowing for versatility in applications. In contrast, anisotropic magnets have enhanced magnetic properties along a specific axis, making them ideal for high-performance applications where directionality matters. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive, electronics, and renewable energy, due to their superior magnetic strength and efficiency.</p></p>
