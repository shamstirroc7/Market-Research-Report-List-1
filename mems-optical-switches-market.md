<p><ul><li>MEMS Single-mode Optical Switches</li><li>MEMS Multimode Optical Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS optical switches utilize micro-electromechanical systems to control the routing of optical signals in communication networks. The market is segmented into MEMS single-mode and multimode optical switches. Single-mode optical switches are designed for long-distance communications, utilizing a single light pathway to minimize signal loss and enhance performance. In contrast, multimode optical switches support multiple light paths, making them suitable for shorter distances and applications requiring higher bandwidth. Together, they cater to varied deployment scenarios in telecommunications and data centers.</p></p>
