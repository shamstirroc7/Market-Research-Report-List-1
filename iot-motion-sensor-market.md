<p><p>The IoT motion sensor market encompasses various types, including Passive Infrared (PIR), Microwave, Ultrasound, and Tomography. PIR sensors detect changes in infrared energy from warm bodies, making them ideal for energy-saving applications. Microwave sensors emit microwave signals and measure their reflection, offering greater detection range and versatility. Ultrasound sensors use sound waves to identify motion, providing high precision in diverse environments. Tomography sensors utilize advanced imaging techniques for comprehensive spatial awareness. Other types may include combined technologies or emerging sensors tailored for specific uses.</p></p>
