<p><p>The pets antibiotic treatment solutions market can be categorized into three main types: external rubbing treatment, oral treatment, and injection therapy. External rubbing treatment involves the application of topical antibiotics directly on affected areas, providing localized relief for skin infections. Oral treatment encompasses antibiotics administered through pills or liquids, targeting systemic infections. Injection therapy involves the delivery of antibiotics via injections, offering rapid absorption and targeted treatment for severe or deep-seated infections. Each type serves specific needs based on the pet's condition and treatment requirements.</p></p>
