<p><p>Emission Monitoring Systems are crucial for tracking air pollutants from industrial sources. Predictive Emission Monitoring Systems (PEMS) utilize real-time data and advanced algorithms to forecast emissions based on operating conditions, enabling proactive management. In contrast, Continuous Emission Monitoring Systems (CEMS) provide real-time measurement of emissions through direct sampling and analysis, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. Both systems serve to enhance environmental performance, reduce emissions, and support industries in meeting regulatory requirements while promoting sustainability practices.</p></p>
