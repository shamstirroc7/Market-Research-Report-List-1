Femtosecond lasers emit pulses in the order of quadrillionths of a second, enabling precise material processing and advanced medical applications. Picosecond lasers, with pulse durations in the trillionths of a second, provide enhanced control over thermal effects, making them ideal for applications such as tattoo removal and aesthetic treatments. Both types offer unique advantages in industries like manufacturing, healthcare, and scientific research.</p></p>
