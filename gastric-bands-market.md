<p><p>The gastric bands market comprises two primary types: adjustable and non-adjustable gastric bands. Adjustable gastric bands feature a mechanism that allows for modifications in tightness, enabling tailored restriction of the stomach to manage weight loss effectively. In contrast, non-adjustable gastric bands possess a fixed size, providing a consistent level of restriction without the option for adjustments. Both types aim to support individuals in their weight loss journey, but they differ in flexibility and adaptability to individual needs.</p></p>
