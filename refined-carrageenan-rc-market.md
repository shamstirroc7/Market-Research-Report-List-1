<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Refined Carrageenan (RC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Iota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refined Carrageenan (RC) is a natural thickening agent derived from red seaweed, with three primary types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda. Kappa Carrageenan forms strong gels in the presence of potassium ions, commonly used in dairy products and desserts. Iota Carrageenan creates softer gels with calcium salts, suitable for jellies and soups. Lambda Carrageenan does not gel but thickens, making it ideal for sauces and dairy applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in the food industry, driven by specific functional properties.</p></p>
