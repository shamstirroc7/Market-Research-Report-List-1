<p><p>The corn dry milling products market primarily includes corn grits, cornmeal, corn flour, Distillers Dried Grains with Solubles (DDGS), and ethanol. Corn grits are coarsely ground corn used in snacks and breakfast foods. Cornmeal, a finer grind, is used for baking and recipes. Corn flour is even more finely milled, suitable for gluten-free applications. DDGS, a byproduct of ethanol production, serves as a high-protein animal feed. Lastly, ethanol is produced for fuel and industrial uses, leveraging corn's starch content.</p></p>
