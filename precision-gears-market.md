<p><p>The precision gears market comprises several key types, each with distinct characteristics and applications. Spur gears, known for their simplicity and efficiency, are commonly used in various machines. Helical gears offer smoother operation and higher load capacity due to their angled teeth. Bevel gears facilitate angled motion transfer, ideal for intersecting shafts. Worm gears enable significant torque multiplication and are prevalent in gearboxes. Gear racks convert rotational motion to linear, while "others" encompass specialized designs catering to niche applications.</p></p>
