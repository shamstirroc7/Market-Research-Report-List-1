<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Accelerometer Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE type</li><li>IEPE type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric accelerometer sensors are classified into two main types: PE (Passive) type and IEPE (Integrated Electronics Piezoelectric) type. PE type sensors generate voltage in response to mechanical stress and require external signal conditioning for output. In contrast, IEPE type sensors integrate electronics within the sensor, providing a conditioned signal, which simplifies installation and improves signal quality. Both types are vital for applications such as vibration measurement and structural health monitoring, with distinct features that cater to various industrial needs.</p></p>
