<p><strong>The Mobile Acoustic Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Microphones</li><li>Traditional Array Microphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile acoustic camera market is primarily segmented into MEMS microphones and traditional array microphones. MEMS microphones are compact, offering high sensitivity and integration capabilities for advanced noise-cancellation and spatial audio applications. In contrast, traditional array microphones utilize multiple pickup elements to capture sound from various directions, allowing for enhanced sound localization and clearer audio in dynamic environments. Both types cater to different needs, with MEMS microphones being favored for portable devices and traditional arrays for professional audio capturing solutions.</p></p>
