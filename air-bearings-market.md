<p><strong>The Air Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerostatic Bearings</li><li>Aerodynamic Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air bearings market comprises two main types: aerostatic and aerodynamic bearings. Aerostatic bearings utilize a thin film of pressurized air to support loads, providing high precision and minimal friction, ideal for applications requiring stability and accuracy. In contrast, aerodynamic bearings rely on the dynamics of airflow generated by the relative motion between surfaces, offering lower friction but requiring higher speeds to operate effectively. Both types are essential in reducing wear and enhancing performance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
