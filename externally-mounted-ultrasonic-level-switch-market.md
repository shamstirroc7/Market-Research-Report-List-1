<p><p>Externally Mounted Ultrasonic Level Switches are categorized into Integral Type and Split Type. Integral Type switches feature all components housed within a single unit, offering convenient installation and maintenance. They are ideal for applications with limited space. In contrast, Split Type switches consist of a separate sensor and controller, allowing for greater flexibility in placement and installation in challenging environments. Both types utilize ultrasonic technology to accurately detect liquid levels, ensuring efficient monitoring in various industrial applications.</p></p>
