<p><p>The Avalanche Backpack Market features two primary types: Mono Avalanche Airbags and Dual Avalanche Airbags. Mono Avalanche Airbags use a single inflatable compartment to provide flotation during avalanches, making them lighter and simpler. In contrast, Dual Avalanche Airbags boast two separate compartments that enhance stability and increase the likelihood of remaining on the snow surface. Both designs aim to improve safety for outdoor enthusiasts in avalanche-prone regions, offering varying benefits based on user preference and specific conditions.</p></p>
