<p><ul><li>Separation Via Breaking Pins</li><li>Separation Via Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Safety Breakaway Couplings market is categorized into two primary types based on their separation mechanisms. The first type, Separation Via Breaking Pins, utilizes specially designed pins that shear under excessive force, allowing for safe disconnection to prevent spills or damage. The second type, Separation Via Cable, employs a cable system that snaps under strain, enabling quick release while maintaining integrity in normal conditions. Both systems are crucial for ensuring safety and preventing hazardous incidents in various industrial applications.</p></p>
