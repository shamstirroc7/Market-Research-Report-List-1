<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cardiac Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrocardiogram (ECG) Devices</li><li>Implantable Loop Recorder (ILRs)</li><li>Cardiac Event Monitors</li><li>Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac monitoring market comprises several key device types. Electrocardiogram (ECG) devices capture and analyze heart electrical activity, enabling early detection of abnormalities. Implantable Loop Recorders (ILRs) are small, subcutaneous devices that continuously monitor heart rhythms for extended periods. Cardiac Event Monitors are portable devices used to record heart activity during symptomatic episodes. Cardiac Output Monitoring (COM) devices assess the volume of blood the heart pumps, providing critical insights for managing various cardiovascular conditions. Together, these technologies enhance patient care and outcomes.</p></p>
