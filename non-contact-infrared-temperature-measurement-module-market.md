<p><p>The Non-contact Infrared Temperature Measurement Module market can be categorized into two types based on signal output: Digital Signal and Analog Signal. </p><p>Digital Signal modules provide temperature readings in a numerical format, allowing for easy integration with digital systems and precise data logging. They often offer advanced features like calibration and communication interfaces. </p><p>Analog Signal modules, on the other hand, output continuous voltage or current corresponding to temperature, making them suitable for straightforward monitoring systems. Both types serve distinct applications across industries such as healthcare, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
