<p><ul><li>Sterile Absorbable Surgical Yarns</li><li>Sterile Nonabsorbable Surgical Yarns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterile surgical sutures market is categorized into two main types: sterile absorbable surgical yarns and sterile nonabsorbable surgical yarns. Absorbable sutures are designed to break down naturally in the body, eliminating the need for removal, making them ideal for internal tissues and soft tissue repairs. In contrast, nonabsorbable sutures are made from materials that do not degrade and are used for long-term support in various surgical procedures, often requiring removal once healing is complete. Both types are essential for effective surgical outcomes.</p></p>
