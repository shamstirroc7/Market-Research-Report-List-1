<p><p>The matte coated paper market consists of two main types: single-sided coated paper and double-sided coated paper. Single-sided coated paper features a matte finish on one side, making it ideal for applications where only one side needs to display high-quality print, such as brochures or posters. Conversely, double-sided coated paper has a matte coating on both sides, allowing for printing on both, which is suitable for products like catalogs and books. Both types offer excellent print quality and a smooth finish.</p></p>
