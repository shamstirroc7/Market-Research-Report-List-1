<p><strong>The Industrial Lightning Arrester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Type Arrester</li><li>Forced the Valve Type Arrester</li><li>Forced the Zinc Oxide Lightning Arrester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial lightning arrester market includes three main types: tube-type arresters, forced valve-type arresters, and forced zinc oxide lightning arresters. Tube-type arresters use gas-filled tubes to divert electrical surges, providing effective protection. Forced valve-type arresters feature a mechanism that opens during overload conditions to redirect surges. Forced zinc oxide arresters utilize zinc oxide varistors, offering high energy absorption and rapid response to transient surges. Each type is designed to enhance electrical system safety by mitigating lightning and surge risks.</p></p>
