<p><strong>The Smart Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart glass market comprises three primary types: Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD Smart Glass, and PDLC Smart Glass. Electrochromic smart glass changes its tint in response to an electrical voltage, offering energy savings and privacy. SPD (Suspended Particle Device) smart glass adjusts its transparency quickly, providing instant control over light and heat. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) smart glass shifts between transparent and opaque when voltage is applied, ensuring privacy and glare reduction. Each type serves varied applications in architectural and automotive sectors.</p></p>
