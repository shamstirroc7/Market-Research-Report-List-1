<p><p>The fiber collimators market is primarily categorized into single mode fiber pigtails and multimode fiber pigtails. Single mode fiber pigtails are designed for long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecom applications. In contrast, multimode fiber pigtails support shorter distances and are commonly used for local area networks and data centers. The selection between these types depends on the specific requirements of data transmission, including distance, bandwidth, and application type.</p></p>
