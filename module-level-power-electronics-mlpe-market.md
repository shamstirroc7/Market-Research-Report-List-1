<p><p>Module Level Power Electronics (MLPE) refers to technologies like power optimizers and microinverters that enhance the efficiency and performance of solar photovoltaic (PV) systems. Power optimizers are devices installed on each solar module to maximize energy production by ensuring that each panel operates at its optimal point, improving overall system performance, especially in partially shaded conditions. Microinverters, on the other hand, convert DC to AC at the module level, allowing individual panel management and enhancing energy yield and reliability in varying conditions.</p></p>
