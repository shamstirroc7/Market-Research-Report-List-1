FDRs capture crucial flight parameters such as altitude, speed, and engine performance, providing essential data for accident investigation and improving flight safety. Both devices play a pivotal role in enhancing aviation safety, compliance with regulations, and facilitating thorough investigations following incidents.</p></p>
