<p><strong>The Offline Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Mode</li><li>Voltage Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offline controllers market is categorized into Current Mode and Voltage Mode. Current Mode controllers regulate output by monitoring the current through the transformer primary, providing fast response to changes in load, enhancing stability, and improving efficiency. Conversely, Voltage Mode controllers adjust the output based on voltage feedback, simplifying design and reducing component count but potentially facing stability issues under certain conditions. Both modes serve distinct applications, balancing performance, complexity, and cost in power supply designs.</p></p>
