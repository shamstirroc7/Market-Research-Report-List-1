<p><strong>The Automotive-grade Intelligent Safety Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"MCU（Microcontroller Unit）"</li><li>"HSM（Hardware Security Module）"</li><li>"TPM（Trusted Platform Module）"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive-grade intelligent safety chip market comprises three key types: </p><p>1. **MCU (Microcontroller Unit)** - These are integrated circuits designed to manage and control specific functions in vehicles, ensuring reliable operation and safety features. </p><p>2. **HSM (Hardware Security Module)** - These units provide cryptographic processing and secure key management to protect vehicle data and communications against unauthorized access.</p><p>3. **TPM (Trusted Platform Module)** - Designed to enhance security, TPMs enable secure hardware-based storage of encryption keys and facilitate secure boot processes, ensuring vehicle integrity.</p></p>
