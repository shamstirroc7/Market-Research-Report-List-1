<p><p>The Biological Indicator (BI) market for steam and hydrogen peroxide sterilization includes two main types: Self-Contained Biological Indicators (SCBIs) and Biological Indicator Strips. SCBIs are compact units that contain a growth medium and spores, providing immediate results post-sterilization. In contrast, Biological Indicator Strips are simpler, flat strips infused with spores that require a separate incubation process for result evaluation. Both types play a crucial role in validating sterilization processes, ensuring the effectiveness of sterilization methods in healthcare and laboratory settings.</p></p>
