<p><p>Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) systems comprise different market types, including Classical TLC, High-Performance TLC (HPTLC), and Preparative TLC (PLC). Classical TLC involves traditional techniques for separating compounds on a coated plate. HPTLC utilizes advanced methods for higher resolution and speed, allowing for improved sensitivity and reproducibility. Preparative TLC focuses on isolating and purifying larger quantities of analytes, making it suitable for preparative purposes in laboratories. Each type serves unique applications in research, pharmaceuticals, and analytical chemistry.</p></p>
