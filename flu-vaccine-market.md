<p><strong>The Flu Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trivalent Influenza Vaccine</li><li>Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flu vaccine market is primarily divided into two types: trivalent and quadrivalent influenza vaccines. Trivalent vaccines are designed to protect against three strains of the virus, typically two influenza A strains and one B strain. In contrast, quadrivalent vaccines offer broader protection by targeting four strains, including two A strains and two B strains. The quadrivalent vaccines are becoming increasingly popular due to their enhanced efficacy and ability to cover more circulating virus strains, leading to improved public health outcomes.</p></p>
