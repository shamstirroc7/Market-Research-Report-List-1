<p><p>The manganese sputtering target market comprises two main types: rotary and non-rotatable. Rotary sputtering targets are designed to spin during the sputtering process, ensuring uniform deposition and extended target life, ideal for high-volume production. Non-rotatable targets, on the other hand, remain fixed, typically used for applications requiring precision over a smaller surface area. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs, with rotary types favored for efficiency and non-rotatable types preferred for specialized, high-accuracy tasks in semiconductor and coating industries.</p></p>
