<p><p>The connector splice market can be categorized into single mode and multimode connector splices. Single mode connector splices are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a narrow core to transmit signals with minimal loss and high bandwidth. In contrast, multimode connector splices offer a larger core diameter, enabling multiple light paths, making them suitable for short-distance applications like data centers and local area networks. Both types are essential for ensuring efficient fiber optic connectivity in various telecommunications and data transmission settings.</p></p>
