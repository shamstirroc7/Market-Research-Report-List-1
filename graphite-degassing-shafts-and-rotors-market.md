<p><p>Graphite degassing shafts and rotors are essential components used in metal casting processes to remove impurities and gas bubbles from molten metal. Degassing shafts typically feature a long, cylindrical design that facilitates the introduction of inert gases into the molten metal, promoting the release of dissolved gases. Rotors, on the other hand, are often integrated into the degassing system to enhance agitation, ensuring better dispersion of the inert gas and improved removal of contaminants. Both types are critical for achieving high-quality metal products.</p></p>
