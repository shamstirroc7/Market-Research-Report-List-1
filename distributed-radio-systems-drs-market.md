<p><p>Distributed Radio Systems (DRS) enhance wireless communication by improving signal distribution. The market is divided into two main types: indoor and outdoor. Indoor DRS focuses on enhancing coverage within buildings, such as offices and malls, utilizing small cells and antennas to combat signal loss from walls and obstacles. Outdoor DRS, on the other hand, aims to extend coverage over larger areas, like urban environments and highways, using macro cells and advanced technologies to ensure robust, wide-ranging connectivity.</p></p>
