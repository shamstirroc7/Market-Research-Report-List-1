Minimally invasive treatment includes procedures like ureteroscopy and percutaneous nephrolithotomy, which involve small incisions for direct stone removal. Other market segments may encompass alternative therapies, dietary management, and pharmaceuticals aimed at preventing stone formation or alleviating symptoms, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
