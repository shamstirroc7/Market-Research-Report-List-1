<p><strong>The Industrial Fire Sprinklers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Sprinkler</li><li>Wet Sprinkler</li><li>Deluge Sprinkler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial fire sprinkler market is categorized into three primary types: dry, wet, and deluge sprinklers. Wet sprinklers are the most common, featuring water-filled pipes that activate when heat is detected. Dry sprinklers use pressurized air or nitrogen to keep water at bay until triggered, ideal for cold environments. Deluge sprinklers have open heads connected to a water supply that releases large amounts of water when activated, suitable for high-hazard areas requiring immediate fire suppression. Each type caters to specific environmental and safety needs.</p></p>
