<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power Assisted Steering (PAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Assisted Steering (PAS) enhances vehicle steering control and maneuverability by utilizing electronic or hydraulic systems. The market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering) features an electric motor integrated into the steering column, offering precise control with minimal weight.</p><p>2. P-EPS (Pumps Electric Power Steering) uses an electric motor to drive a hydraulic pump for steering assistance, combining traditional hydraulic systems with electric efficiency.</p><p>3. R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) integrates the electric motor directly into the steering rack, providing improved response and efficiency by minimizing energy loss.</p></p>
